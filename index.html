Get to know Us More.

At KVK, we combine academic excellence, innovative teaching, and hands-on experience to prepare our students for success in a dynamic global environment. Founded in 2002, KVK is one of Lithuania’s leading institutions for practical, career-focused higher education.
The university offers programs across various levels of higher education, including undergraduate (bachelor’s) and professional studies,preparing students for success in a dynamic global environment. Our community brings together students, educators, and professionals, fostering creativity, collaboration, and cultural exchange.
Whether you are beginning your studies, exploring new opportunities, or continuing your professional development KVK is the place where your future begins.

What makes Us Different

  • Hands-on learning: Students gain real-world experience through projects, labs, and internships.
  • International community: Diverse students and staff create a collaborative and culturally rich environment.
  • Career-focused programs: The curriculum is designed to equip students with skills that are in demand in today’s job market.
